Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 12, 2025

At Attendly, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains our unique approach to data and how we protect your information.

The Core Principle: We Don't Store Your Data

Attendly is fundamentally different from typical Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) products. The entire system, including the codebase (Google Apps Script) and the data storage (Google Sheets), is designed to be installed and run directly within your own Google account.

Because of this architecture:

  • We never see your data. Your employees' names, punch-in/out times, and all other attendance data are stored in a Google Sheet that you own and control. This data never passes through or is stored on our servers.
  • We have no access to your Google account. The installation process involves you copying our code and deploying it yourself. We do not require access to your account at any point.
  • Your data is protected by Google's security. All your information is protected by the same world-class security measures that Google uses to protect its own data.

Information We Do Collect

The only information we collect is related to the purchase transaction itself and for providing customer support.

  • Purchase Information: When you purchase Attendly, our payment processor (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) will collect payment information required to complete the transaction. We receive confirmation of the purchase, which may include your name and email address, but we do not receive or store your credit card details.
  • Support Inquiries: If you contact us for support via email, we will use your email address to communicate with you and resolve your issue. We keep a record of support correspondence.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for:

  • Processing your one-time purchase.
  • Providing you with customer support and installation assistance.
  • Sending you important information about product updates, if any.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is only shared with essential service providers like payment processors as required to complete your purchase.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
