Our Mission

To empower small businesses with simple, affordable, and secure tools that give them back their most valuable asset: time.

The Story Behind Attendly

Attendly was born out of a common frustration. As small business owners ourselves, we grew tired of the endless cycle of monthly subscription fees for software that was often too complicated for our needs. We wanted a time tracking solution that was powerful yet simple, secure yet accessible. We looked everywhere, but couldn't find it. So, we decided to build it.

We realized that millions of businesses already use and trust Google Workspace. The infrastructure is robust, secure, and for most, completely free. Why not build a solution on top of that? That's when the idea for Attendly sparked to life: a one-time purchase time clock that runs entirely within your own Google account.

Our Philosophy

We believe in a few core principles:

  • ✓
    Ownership Matters: You should own your software, not rent it. A one-time purchase eliminates financial uncertainty and puts you in control.
  • ✓
    Simplicity is Power: The best tools are the ones that are easy to use. We designed Attendly to be intuitive for both managers and employees, with zero training required.
  • ✓
    Your Data is Yours: We have a steadfast commitment to data privacy. With Attendly, your sensitive employee data never touches our servers. It stays securely in your Google account, where it belongs.

Thank you for being a part of our journey to simplify time tracking for businesses everywhere.
